What is 2dcl?

2dcl aims to be Decentraland's 2d alternative universe.

What are the goals of 2dcl?

  • Testing the ability of the Decentraland protocol to support alternative clients.
  • Creating an accessible ecosystem for people to create 2d experiences in Decentraland.
  • Experimenting with alternative implementations/protocols from what the Foundation is doing.

See the proposal for more info

What's the current status?

  • You can walk through Decentraland in 2d. Streets and parcels are recognized.
  • You can create a scene to deploy in a parcel you own.
  • You can add your eth address and see your pixelated avatar.
  • You can check the deployed scenes.
  • Very experimental status, everything is expected to break.

What's coming?

We're working on a new set of updates thanks to the Decentraland DAO grants program:

  • Metamask integration for Login and Deployment
  • Content discovery database (in-game and website)
  • View and communicate with other users
